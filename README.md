# wesbite-design-with-wordpress-ebook
Website Design With WordPress is a comprehensive educational guide and training program created by Endy Edeson, a Lagos-based ICT instructor, web developer, and founder of Edeson InfoTech Global Services. The offering exists both as a published physical/digital guide and an in-person or distance learning course designed to teach absolute beginners how to build professional websites and monetize their technical skills.
The Guide & Book Details:
The material is distributed as a detailed guidebook available on Selar and Amazon. It breaks down core developmental phases including:
Core WordPress Architecture: Customizing themes, working with plug-ins, and managing post setups.
Web Architecture Fundamentals: Understanding domain registration, SSL certificates, custom webmail creation, and database backups.
Industry Alternatives: Side-by-side introductory overviews of Wix, Shopify, Drupal, Joomla, and Dreamweaver.
Monetization: Strategies for freelancing, securing digital marketing jobs, and navigating tech hubs.

Training ProgramsFor individuals seeking practical mentorship, Endy Edeson offers standardized training through the Edeson Tutorials platform. Courses are structured as follows:
Delivery Formats: Available via distance learning, home lessons, or physical classes based at Ikotun, Lagos State.
Course Duration: Typically structured as 2-hour long modules spanning sessions held three times a week.
Credentials: A verified Document of Completion / Certificate is issued by Edeson InfoTech Global Services upon final project submission.
Support Channels: Direct student onboarding and service inquiries are handled explicitly through the Edeson WhatsApp Channel
